Shipping & Delivery

At Fetch Mobility, we are committed to ensuring timely and secure delivery of your orders. This policy outlines the terms and conditions for shipping and delivery, which may vary depending on order volume, location, and customization requirements.

1. Shipping Terms

• Order Confirmation:

- Orders are processed and confirmed upon receipt of an approved Purchase Order and advance payment as per our Upfront Sale Model terms.

- Any changes to the order, including volume or customization, must be communicated before production begins.

• Shipping Method:

- Products are shipped using reliable logistics partners, selected based on destination and order specifications.

- Shipping methods and timelines will be communicated during order confirmation.

2. Delivery Timeframes

• Delivery timelines depend on:

- Order Volume: Larger orders may require additional production and handling time.

- Customization: Orders requiring special customization may take longer to fulfill.

- Location: Delivery times vary based on the geographical location of the client. Remote areas may require additional transit time.

Estimated delivery timelines will be provided after the order is confirmed.

3. Shipping Costs

• Shipping charges are calculated based on order volume, destination, and any special requirements (e.g., expedited shipping).

• Shipping costs will be detailed in the Purchase Order or invoice provided to the client.

4. Ownership and Risk

• Ownership of products transfers to the client upon full payment and delivery.

• Fetch Mobility is not liable for delays caused by unforeseen circumstances, including natural disasters, transportation issues, or customs delays.

5. Delivery Inspection

• Clients are required to inspect the delivery upon receipt.

• Any discrepancies, damages, or defects must be reported to Fetch Mobility within 10 business days of delivery.

6. International Shipping

• For orders outside India, clients are responsible for any additional fees, including customs duties, taxes, or import regulations.

• Delivery timelines for international orders may vary depending on customs clearance procedures.
